About the Creator

Hi! My Name is Caitlin Callihan and I have been playing League of Legends since I was 13 years old. I have been playing Nami for 11 years. This character has been a defining part of my childhood and has followed me since. League of Legends is not a video game, but an esport, racking in views that beat the super bowl internationally every year. It is a hobby, sport, that involves mental dexterity similar to chess with all of its moving pieces although at first glance you cannot tell. I currently work as a secretary, but on the side I enjoy taking classes that teach me more about computers and coding. I hope to one day work with what I enjoy.
